ABOUT NOVA

We Are Building
More Than AI.
We Are Building A Friend.

NOVA is a friendly AI companion designed to support, listen, motivate and understand people. Our mission is simple — to make technology feel human by creating an AI that genuinely cares for its users.

Our Story

We are a group of four passionate BCA students from Dr. Virendra Swarup Institute of Computer Studies.

During our academic journey, we wanted to create something that could positively impact people's lives.

That's how NOVA was born — an AI companion designed not only to answer questions but also to provide emotional support, motivation, and meaningful conversations.

We believe technology should not replace human emotions, but rather help people feel heard, understood, and supported whenever they need it.
